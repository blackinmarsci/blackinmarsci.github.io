I’m a wildlife Biologist and Science Communicator I have a passion for nature and evening bigger passion for sharing and immersing the younger generation into nature, especially BIPOC who may not picture themselves in the marine field. I’m from Austell Georgia and I’m currently pursuing a master’s degree Studying Seaside Sparrows and Insect dynamics in Tidal marshes of Georgia. I have held several Positions in the marine Field including: Environmental Educator, Sea Turtle Biologist and Endangered Species Observer. I enjoy working in the marine field because many of the animals and their environment teaches us to go with the flow, life is full of changes you can either give up or flow with the waves.
