I am an early career shark scientist interested in shark biomechanics, functional morphology, and conservation. I graduated from Rutgers University in May 2020 with a degree in marine biology. I am currently applying to graduate schools with the hopes of continuing my studies in sharks science. I enjoy marine science because there is an endless road discovering new things about the ocean and the animals that live in it. I specifically enjoy shark research because it combines my love of hands on experiences with my curiosity of sharks and how they interact with their environment.
